Bloom's Taxonomy

It is a model that categorizes learning objectives into varying levels of complexity, from remembering to creating things.

Working Principle

Generate questions of different difficulty level.

We use Bloom's Taxonomy as difficulty level categorization's core. QnsAI is made with teachers in mind. The ML and NLP algorithms work how a teacher’s mind would work when creating a test paper. There is option to choose different difficulty level.

Easy

This level involves the retrieval of factual information as a learning objective.

Medium

This level mostly involves action, display, relation, execution, etc.

Hard

This level mostly involves creation of designs and self-solving of problems.

How QnsAI works?

Steps Involved in the Implementation Process

Submitting Data

We allow different way for inputting data like Copy/ Paste Text, PDF files, MS Word Docx, YouTube URL, Images and even from Wikipedia searches

Keyword Dectetion

The natural language text is analyzed using ANN (Artificial Neural Networks) and dectects the keywords. You also have option to add/remove keywords

Forming Questions

Different kinds of questions are formed at different difficulty level according to user's preferences

Final Paper

After verifying quesions are right and after adding your own questions, the questions can be exported to multiple formats like PDF, Word or QTI.
